may 25 22 11:44 am

he hadn't touched me
not ever
and yet he didn't need to
his presence
the fact that he was there
was more than enough
he isn't here anymore
not anymore
it hasn't been long
yet
his absence
such a profound loss
as if he had held me for so long
pulling away, leaving, leaving
i think of him more than myself
i think of him more than anything
he is everything, more than, even
and yet
i have felt so little of him
known so little
like a drizzling rain
or a evangelical whisper
he is so dear to me despite his hideous nothing
i feel empty
not a drop of water inside me to try to see
i am simply an empty glass
i will never have his love to keep
nothing to fill that him-shaped hole
in my heart
i've never felt this way before
never have i felt so alone
missing a touch i have never felt
i miss him, i want to say
but what is there to miss
you can't miss nothing
can you?

10:28 pm apr 9 22

i fell in love with him the way you fall asleep
a little bit, then all at once
i used to believe that love was impossible
so, so impossible, so difficult
yet, it still goes on and went on and on
despite my beliefs.
one sided love is perhaps the only love
that could ever come my way
a delusional, manic kind
with an air of erotic insanity
with open arms i claw my way through it
and i come out thinking i am free
until i fall back through again
back to where it always begins
a passing glance and winding red
until i am nothing but yearning

7:31 pm apr 7 22

want was tearing his flesh off
and eating him alive
crawling inside of his ribcage
adoring every inch inside and out
love him, i do, i carnally desire him
cupping his face and kissing him whole
feeling glass break against glass
bruising hips and thighs
his vocal cords snap in broken chords
glorious, i think, seeing you smile
your gaze brushing my jawline
my insanity is almost erotic
a breath of mania in the air
as i imagine your teeth against mine
your hands ripping into my back
my own clawing your collarbone
this is momentary forgiveness;
longing, loneliness, yearning,
all of it erased in your last glance

10:03pm mar 23 22

i've always wondered,
why do bad things keep happening to me?
what did i do to deserve this? why, do i deserve this?
it's like when i feel that i'm at my lowest,
suddenly the world turns against me, and
i am alone. i am hopeless, helpless, tasteless for acting
and believing the notion that these bad things are a punishment
from some angry universal force.
why do these things keep happening to me?

cruel, like hateful cold creeping up a back
in the midst of winter, killing you slowly
i am empty, cruel and empty of all feeling
if i cannot be loved then i will be feared

cherry red perfume stains my lungs
a deep, sultry, sweet gourmand
why i feel this way i do not know
you leave the taste of regret in my mouth
now all i know is regret and regret more
cherry sweet and bitter and cold
red, a deep glowing red, lust and anger
swirling deep in the pits of my stomach
drizzled with a tasteless chypre.

messy poems


10:07pm mar 20 22

cherry red perfume stains my lungs
a deep, sultry, sweet gourmand
why i feel this way i do not know
you leave the taste of regret in my mouth
now all i know is regret and regret more
cherry sweet and bitter and cold
red, a deep glowing red, lust and anger
swirling deep in the pits of my stomach
drizzled with a tasteless chypre.
